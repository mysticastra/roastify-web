Delete Your Account

Last updated: April 6, 2026

How to delete your account

Log in to Roastify, open your account settings, and tap Delete Account.

What data will be deleted

When deletion is requested, we permanently remove:

  • Your profile and account identifiers.
  • Your roast history and related database records.
  • Your app-specific settings linked to your account.

Deletion timeline

Account deletion is completed within 24 hours of your request.

Need help?

If you face issues deleting your account, contact hello@pawanrajbhatta.com.np.
